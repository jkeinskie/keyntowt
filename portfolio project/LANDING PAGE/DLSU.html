About us:

Welcome to De Lubag State University, where excellence meets opportunity! Established with a vision to nurture holistic education and empower future leaders, De Lubag State University (DLSU) stands as a beacon of academic excellence in Brgy. Lubag, Enchanted, Leyte.

DLSU is committed to providing a dynamic learning environment that fosters intellectual growth, critical thinking, and personal development. With a diverse range of programs spanning across various disciplines, we strive to meet the educational needs of our students and prepare them for success in their chosen fields.

Our dedicated faculty members are experts in their respective fields, passionate about teaching, and committed to providing students with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in today's competitive world. Through innovative teaching methods, hands-on learning experiences, and cutting-edge research opportunities, we aim to inspire our students to reach their full potential and become lifelong learners.

At DLSU, we believe in the importance of community engagement and social responsibility. Through various outreach programs and partnerships with local organizations, we encourage our students to make a positive impact on society and contribute to the welfare of their communities.

Whether you are a prospective student, a current student, an alumna/alumnus, or a member of the wider community, we invite you to join us on our journey of academic excellence and personal growth at De Lubag State University.

Welcome to DLSU, where your future begins!
